About

I was born in Seoul, South Korea but my family immigrated to California when I was just 3 years old. Even though I spent a majority of my life in the US and only attended American schools, I am still fluent in Korean as it is my birth tongue and the language we use at home. At my internship at 3i Inc. in Seoul last summer, I had no problems communicating in Korean, even in a formal work setting.

After graduating from high school in 2018, I attended the University of Washington-Seattle as a pre-science major, which is essentially undeclared but within the sciences field. During my sophomore year, I applied to the UW Informatics major as well as to transfer admission to University of California schools just to open up my options. Though I was accepted to the UW iSchool Informatics major, I ended up transferring to the University of California-Davis as a Computer Science major after taking multiple factors into consideration.

Currently, I am a Silicon Valley, CA based software developer and recent graduate from UC Davis with a B.S. in Computer Science. I am seeking full-time employment as a full-stack software developer / engineer. In my free time, I am studying deeper computer science topics and practicing the skills I learn in the form of small personal projects
